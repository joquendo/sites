My favorite animal

Wolves

My favorite animal right now is a wolf. Wolves are loyal and have a natural instinct to protect. There is nothing more passionate about an animal than its self-sacrifice to protect another. Wolves are also intellegent pack animals, understanding that there is strength in numbers. They roam together, eat together, and live together. This behavior shows the evolving primal instinct of caring, belonging, and loyalty.
